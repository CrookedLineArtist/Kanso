Property management and rental platform

Homzhub is a platform mainly for NRIs or other investors who own properties in India but do not live in the same town or country. It allows them to remotely list the property either for management or rental.

Brief

The client had already developed a initial prototype based on their vision but they felt it lacked face value and intuitiveness. The brief required us to restructure the existing interface to make it more usable and intuitive along and develop a modern visual language.

User flows

Upon closer inspection we observed that their prototype had major experience drawbacks. Flows were broken frustrating the user. New user task flows were created based on the goals. This helped us understand the functioning of the product better as we pushed to make each process as smooth as possible.

Wireframes

With the acquired knowledge of the user journies the information architecture and interactions for each step had to be created. As the platform was information heavy, we aimed at restructuring the data and provide it to the user in smaller chunks which were relevant to him. While this imrpoved scanability to ensured that the user would not drop out.

Top vs side navigation

With a client adamant on having a side navigation system we strived for a better understanding. Our platform was more focused with limited topics of interest. A traditional top navigation is easier to scan and use. Thus being better suited.

Output

The visual language would reflect their brand colours and evoked trust, transparency and professionalism. More over it aimed at providing a clutter free and clean experience.

Post a property with ease

The most crucial and longest process to the portal made easy. All information is fed to the user in small chunks to make it seem simple and short. These chunks help maintain the focus on particular information group.

Seemless owner -tenant connect

Mainly used by people with off shore properties, it required a communication between owner and tenant. An inbuilt widget system enabled that. The tenant and owner could connect and schedule visits or negotiate rent without having to ever call each other.

Comprehensive dashboard

An owner can keep track of the properties and the finances. The tenant can keep a track of the viewed properties and later also keep a track of the finances of a rented one.
